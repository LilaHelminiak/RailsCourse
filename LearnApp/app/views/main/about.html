What is Learn App?
Learn App will make it easier for you to learn new words and definitions

How does it works?
After you create your account, you will have the possibility to create your own list of words that you want to learn.
Then you can test yourself. You pick a category and a quiz that contains random vocabulary/definition is drawn for you.
If your answer is wrong, the word will appear in the future with higher probability,
but if you correctly match a word with its definition, the probability for drawing this word in a quiz will be smaller.
